The signs your Go-To-Market motion
is starting to crack

Most founders hit these walls between £2M and £8M ARR. They're not signs of failure — they're signs of growth outpacing your operating model.

Pipeline inconsistency

Some months are great, others aren't — and no one really knows why. You're still running on founder energy, not system.

Founder dependency

Deals still need you to close. The team can't replicate what you do. You're a bottleneck in your own revenue motion.

No single source of truth

Data is scattered across tools. Decisions get made on gut feeling, not signal. Forecasting is optimism dressed as analysis.

The headcount trap

The instinct is to hire more people. But more people don't fix a broken system — they amplify the problem and multiply the cost.
